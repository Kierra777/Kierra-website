Is there anything better than the delicious duo of rich milk chocolate and smooth peanut butter? We think not.

Reese's was introduced by H.B. Reese in 1928 when he created the classic Peanut Butter Cups in his basement. At the time, they were sold as part of a variety of assorted candies and were not a stand-alone product. They gained popularity, and the H.B. Reese Candy Company was established.

Welcome to the World of Reese's

It’s no secret that REESE’S Peanut Butter Cups are one of the best candies around. Did you know REESE’S Cups were actually invented by a former Hershey employee Harry Burnett Reese?

Mr. Hershey supported Reese’s venture, even supplying him with the milk chocolate. REESE’S Cups became part of The Hershey Company family when H.B. Reese’s sons sold the business in 1963.
